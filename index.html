vibe before you arrive.

A one-stop app to capture and experience a restaurant’s ambience through trusted customer reviews, mood boards, word clouds, and fit inspiration.

discover

Abou​​t

Many people find it daunting to try new restaurants, often because they’re unsure about whether the ambience of the restaurants matches what they’re looking for. 

That’s where ambi comes in—ambi is all about illustrating the vibes of restaurants through various palatable mediums. From moodboards to outfit inspos to video reviews created by other users, ambi helps users discover and experience the atmosphere of restaurants before they even get there.

Features

Discover New
Vibes

Scroll your feed to see other users' video reviews of restaurants, curated to your location/interests to help you discover new places with the vibes you're looking for.

Experience the
Atmosphere

Restaurants' landing pages feature moodboards, word clouds, soundtracks, fit inspos, and their popular times to help you understand the atmosphere in different ways.

Contribute to the
Community

Post video reviews of restaurants, add to word clouds, and contribute to fit inspos to help others on ambi with their discoveries!
